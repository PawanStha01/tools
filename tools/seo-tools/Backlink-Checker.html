Quick Tips
About Backlinks

Backlinks are links from other websites that point to your site. Search engines use them as a ranking factor.

Quality Over Quantity
  • Focus on getting links from authoritative sites
  • Natural link profiles have a mix of follow/nofollow
  • Relevant links from industry sites are most valuable
How to Use This Tool
  1. Enter your website URL
  2. Optionally add an API key for detailed metrics
  3. Click "Check Backlinks"
  4. Review the results and identify opportunities
Note

This tool provides simulated results. For comprehensive backlink analysis, consider using professional SEO tools like Ahrefs, Moz, or SEMrush.
