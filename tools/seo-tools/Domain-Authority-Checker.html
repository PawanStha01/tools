About Domain Authority
What is Domain Authority?

Domain Authority (DA) is a search engine ranking score developed by Moz that predicts how well a website will rank on search engine result pages (SERPs).

DA Score Ranges
  • 80-100 (Excellent) Top Tier
  • 60-79 (Good) Strong
  • 40-59 (Fair) Average
  • 0-39 (Poor) Needs Work
How to Improve Your DA
  • Build high-quality backlinks
  • Create valuable, shareable content
  • Improve your site's technical SEO
  • Optimize for mobile and page speed
