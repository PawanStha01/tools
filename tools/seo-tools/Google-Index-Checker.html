About Google Indexing
Why Check Index Status?

Google indexing means your pages are stored in Google's database and can appear in search results. If pages aren't indexed, they won't rank.

Common Indexing Issues
  • Pages blocked by robots.txt
  • Noindex meta tag present
  • Low-quality or duplicate content
  • Poor internal linking
  • New pages not yet crawled
How to Fix Indexing Problems
  1. Check for "noindex" tags
  2. Submit URLs to Google Search Console
  3. Improve internal linking
  4. Create a sitemap and submit it
  5. Ensure pages load quickly with no errors
