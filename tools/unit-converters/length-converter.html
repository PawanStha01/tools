About Length Units

Length is a measure of distance. In the International System of Units (SI), the base unit of length is the meter (m).

Metric Units
  • Kilometer (km): 1 km = 1,000 meters
  • Meter (m): The base unit of length in the metric system
  • Centimeter (cm): 1 cm = 0.01 meters
  • Millimeter (mm): 1 mm = 0.001 meters
  • Micrometer (μm): 1 μm = 0.000001 meters
  • Nanometer (nm): 1 nm = 0.000000001 meters
Imperial/US Units
  • Mile (mi): 1 mi = 1,609.344 meters
  • Yard (yd): 1 yd = 0.9144 meters
  • Foot (ft): 1 ft = 0.3048 meters
  • Inch (in): 1 in = 0.0254 meters
Other Units
  • Nautical Mile (nmi): 1 nmi = 1,852 meters (used in marine and air navigation)
